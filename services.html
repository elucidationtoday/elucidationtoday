Problem Solving and Design Thinking

The secret to innovation.


Why is problem-solving and design thinking relevant?

Problem-Solving has remained relevant throughout the history of humankind, leading to significant reforms in work and ways of living. Design Thinking is a problem-solving protocol emphasizing on the consumer, questioning assumptions and redefining problems to achieve beneficial innovations. It has become very important in building products and in problem-solving for users in recent times.
An amalgamation of logic and creativity, Problem Solving & Design Thinking is emerging as one of the prerequisites for stepping into the corporate world, with top companies adopting it as part of their strategies and hiring process. With this in mind, universities across the globe have rolled out courses in itto prepare generations to come.


What We Offer

When it comes to changing perceptions and imparting problem-solving skills at any workplace, Elucidation Today is a one-stop solution. We prefer providing an understanding of the problem-solving and design thinking framework right at the school or college level, moulding individuals well before they join the professional world. We conduct problem-solving workshops at schools and colleges by introducing the concept of design thinking through group activities. The group activities are designed to demonstrate the processes of addressing problems that are visible in our surroundings, redefining them and brainstorming possible solutions or alternatives. Throughout the process, students are guided by mentors from the Elucidation Today team.


Why should you choose Elucidation Today?

Elucidation Today addresses the need for awareness around Problem Solving & Design Thinking in India, and wishes to impart its expertise on these skills so as to instill a sense of innovation in the youth of our country. We aim to change perceptions around problem-solving methods to help them function efficiently in their field and lead them to achieve greater heights.
